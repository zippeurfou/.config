Each sprint is of 1 week and follow this naming convention: "{YEAR} PERS {month/day start} - {month/day end}"

Here are a few to give you an idea:
2025 PERS 09/05 - 09/11
2025 PERS 09/12 - 09/18
2025 PERS 09/19 - 09/25

Each sprints starts at 5 AM of the start day and finish at 11.30 PM of the end day.

First find what is the sprint that start and finish the latest that has been created. Then create 5 subsequent sprint for it with the proper start and end date.
